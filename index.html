What is this animation?

The animation on this page is a representation of a multiplication table of a given factor (center-bottom) in terms of a given base (center-top). We can call these F and B respectively. The animation iterates over the natural numbers in order starting from one. These numbers up to and including the base value B are drawn at equidistant points around the cicumference of the circle. The first line drawn is 1 x F so it connects the 1st point on the circle to the Fth point. The second line drawn is 2 x F so it connects the 2nd point to the 2Fth point, and so on. If the product is greater than the value of the base, then the circular 'number-line' represented by the circle will continue by wrapping around and the (B + 1)th point will be represented by the 1st point.
